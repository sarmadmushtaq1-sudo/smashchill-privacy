Privacy Policy for Smash & Chill

Last updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile game Smash & Chill ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use it. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

Smash & Chill does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

The App stores your game progress (such as points, unlocked items, and level stars) locally on your device. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, a service operated by Google LLC. To serve ads, Google AdMob and its partners may collect and use certain information, including device identifiers (such as advertising identifiers), general location derived from IP address, device and usage information, and interaction with ads.

This information is used to deliver, measure, and improve advertising. You can learn how Google uses information from apps that use its services here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On Apple iOS devices, the App will ask for your permission before tracking your activity for advertising purposes, in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You may choose to allow or deny this request, and you can change your choice at any time in your device's Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking. If you do not allow tracking, you will still see ads, but they may be less relevant to you.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us so we can address it.

Your Choices

You can reset or limit ad personalization through your device settings:

  • On iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, and Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising.
  • On Android: Settings > Google > Ads.

You may also clear locally stored game data by deleting and reinstalling the App.

Data Retention

Game progress is stored only on your device and persists until you delete the App or clear its data. We do not maintain servers that store your personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
